一些常用函数的导数如下：

$$
[x^k]' = k x^{k-1}
$$

$$
[e^x]' = e^x
$$

$$
[\sin (x)]' = \cos (x)
$$

$$
[\cos (x)]' = \sin (x)
$$

$$
[\ln (x)]' = \frac{1}{x}
$$

$$
[\arctan (x)]' = \frac{1}{1 + x^2}
$$
